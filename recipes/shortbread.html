Vegan Shortbread

Description

Shortbread is one of the most delicious kinds of cookies. They're usually made with lots of butter - but it's not at all a problem to make them vegan. Most important is their melt-in-you-mouth quality. This comes from using powdered sugar.

Ingredients

  • Vegan Butter
  • Powdered Sugar
  • Vanilla Extract
  • Flour
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Beat the vegan butter and powdered sugar in a standmixer.
  2. Add vanilla and salt, keep beating.
  3. Gradually add the flour. Mix well until the dough comes together.
  4. Shape the dough into a brick shape and wrap it in plastic wrap. Chill for 1 hour in the fridge.
  5. Preheat the oven to 180°C
  6. Cut the brick of dough into slices (0,6cm).
  7. Place slices on a cookie sheet lined with parchment paper. Prick the top of the cookies with a fork.
  8. Bake for 10-12 minutes until cookies are set on top but still light colour.
