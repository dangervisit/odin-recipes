Chana Masala (vegan)

Description

Chana Masala is a delicious Indian dish with lots of chickpeas, flavourful spices, and a savoury sauce. Here, we make it vegan.

Ingredients

  • 1 Small Onion
  • 1 Garlic Clove
  • 2cm Ginger
  • ¼ tsp Cinnamon
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • ½ tsp Cumin
  • 1 tsp Coriander
  • 2 Bay Leaves
  • ½ tsp Turmeric
  • ¼ tsp Cayenne Pepper
  • Juice of Half Lemon
  • 2 Cans of Chickpeas
  • 1 Can Coconut Milk
  • Salt & Pepper

Steps

  1. Fry the diced onion and garlic with a little water.
  2. Add the spices and saute briefly.
  3. Add all other ingredients and let it boil briefly.
  4. Serve with rice or chapati.
