Laphet - Burmese Tea Leaf Salad

Image by KamranAydinov on Freepik

Description

Myanmar is the only country where you can not only drink, but also eat tea - that is, fermented tealeaves. Tealeaf salad is the national dish of Myanmar, and very unique in its taste. Besides fermented tealeaves, the salad also includes ingredients such as tomatoes, cabbage, and crunchy nuts.

Ingredients

  • Fermented Tealeaves
  • Crunchy Mix
  • Cabbage
  • Tomatoes
  • Fresh Lime
  • Optional: Oil

Steps

  1. Cut the cabbage into thin stripes.
  2. Cut the tomatoes into small cubes.
  3. Put all ingredients into a bowl.
  4. Mix well. Add lime juice, salt and oil to taste.
